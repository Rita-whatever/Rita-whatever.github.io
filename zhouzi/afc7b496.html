【小剧场】玛丽的故事

发表于 2018-05-22 | 本文阅读量 次
字数统计: 1.4k | 阅读时长 ≈ 6

应付文学课作业弄出来的东西

好俗的追梦故事

现在看来还挺好玩的hh

 

文学终稿
S1: 宿舍(日记本)
S2：食堂
S3：未来Mary的家

 

关于角色形象：
Mary(17岁): 高中校服，头发披下来
Mary（27岁）：头发盘起来，戴着袖套，穿着朴素（家庭主妇形象）也许加个围裙？
两个女主的朋友最好有一点点形象上的反差
Jenny的妈妈：穿的那种带花的衣服，中年妇女的样子。

 

S1:女孩走到桌边坐下，打开日记本
日记本第一页写着名字生日等个人信息
独白与日记本的打开同时进行：
My name is Mary and I’m in grade 10. It is my 16th birthday today. I bought a new diary book since it’s a brand new start for high school. I must work harder this year and I hope I can take a closer step to my dream. （女孩出现，坐下打开日记本）

日记本翻到第一篇，女孩拿起笔开始写字
2017/12/14
I told my best friends about my dream today, but…女孩抬起头进入回忆（淡入淡出）

 

S2：操场看台
G1: I read the notice and found myself in Kappa. Which classes are you guys in?
G2: Oh-no. Not the same one. I’m in Omega.
M: I…..uh……I’m in Delta.
G1: Delta? Wait, you chose physic 1 and 2?
G2: You chose physic 1 and 2? Why? You won’t choose physic as a major, will you?
M: Actually, I want to study engineering in the future.
沉默(G1G2互相交替眼神，摇摇头，表示难以理解)
G1: Ha….hahahaha. Come on, we all know that you will choose history as a major.
女孩低下了头, 没有接话
G2：Oh this is not a joke. Oh god, you are serious!

 

S1: 日记本（放下笔，伤心的走到走廊上）
What’s the problem with my dream? My classmates gave me a disbelieving stare just because I chose physics 1 and 2. Even my friends consider it as a joke. Yes, it is my fault to get an F in physic the last semester, although I did my best. But is it also my fault that I want to be an engineer? Maybe, maybe I should just give up…….

 

S3：（未来）餐桌旁，未来的Mary在一张写着To the ten-year younger self的纸张上写着
To: 2017
From: 2027
Believe in yourself! Just study physic and choose engineering as a major. Everything will be fine in the future.

写完这一句后，传来开门的声音和小孩的喊叫声
Child (only voice): Mom, starving!
Mary 放下笔从桌子旁站: I’m coming, darling.

 

S1：Mary第二天醒来在日记本上发现了那行字，字的末尾还写着from 2027，她抱着困惑走出宿舍。
一边从宿舍走出来,一边独白：Who wrote this? The writing style is so familiar. It can’t be my friends as they found my dream ridiculous. But I didn’t tell anyone else……

S1: 日记本
2017/12/23
My teachers, my mom and dad always say “whenever you decide to work hard, it’s never too late. Believe in yourself and you will succeed”. But as I got to high school I found out that these words are just tricks to please little child. Nobody believes me.

Mary在宿舍和妈妈打电话
J：Mom……….(带着一点忧伤的语气)
M（非常激动,语速很快）:Jenny my darling, you don’t know how hard it is to be an engineer! Your Uncle John, remember? He had only slept 2 hours a day in order to finish his paper. And he is a BOY！I don’t want my little girl to be so stressful and tired.
J: But I really find engineering intere…….
M: Okay, okay, we will talk about it later. You should consider it carefully. My boss is calling me. Mummy love you, bye~

第二天，又有一行字出现了
I’m yourself from 2027. I know all about you, your struggle, your choice, and your final success. I experienced all of these. Don’t blame your parents cause they don’t want you to take the risk. But don’t be afraid, my girl, I will keep in touch with you and give you some advice. You WILL become an engineer.

合上日记本，一个同学走过
G: Have you decided yet?
M: YES.

 

独：That’s it. Believe it or not, I’m chatting with my future self on my dairy. We have kept in touch for two years and I learned more about her. “She” is a successful engineer now. She hasn’t reached her thirties but she has already designed two wonderful buildings in our city. I’m now in grade 12 and I am ready to begin my university application. I can’t tell whether I am still susceptible of others’ opinion, but there’s one thing I’m sure now…

BGM响起来，最好是那种循序渐进的感觉
Mary到达图书馆，将一本一本书从书架上抽出来（镜头给一个书名的特写）
Mary在大学的官网上查找Engineering 专业
Mary 找同学讨论物理问题

未来的Mary坐在桌前合上笔记本，特写桌面上的图纸和笔记本的封面。

黑底白字：
Life is only once
Do what you want
Maybe you don’t have a future guide
But you have yourself

 

G2: Have you heard that Zaha Hadid passed away last week?
G1: WHO? (chuckle a bit) I haven’t seen any R.I.P. on the wechat…
G2:oh, haha
G1: Sorry, go on, who is she?
M: 冷静的突然接话。One of the most eminent architect in modern world. Also the first woman received the Pritzker Architecture Prize.
G2:Yeah
G1: Uh……not really my area kind of thing……
G2: The Galaxy SOHO in Beijing and Guangzhou Opera House are also her projects!
M: Have you been to those places?
G2: Yeah, It’s funny that I have took pictures of those extraordinary buildings without knowing who built them.
M: There are always some people being acknowledged only at the time they died. How distinguished an architect she is!
G1: Yes
G2: That’s true
M: I’m just…… I’m just wondered that why people always believe that girls can’t pursue an engineering career. There are just so many evidence showing that women can also perfectly competent for this job.
G1: Maybe you are right

 

（如果有位置可以加的话。。。。）一句话心理活动：
Would I spare no effort to pursue my dream, one day I will be as successful as Zaha Hadid…… or the future me, won’t I?
